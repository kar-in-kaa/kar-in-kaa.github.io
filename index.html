Born in Slovakia and raised there until my early teens, I’ve since moved through Brazil, Hong Kong, the Netherlands and Indonesia. Now, I landed back in Amsterdam. My personal and artistic perspective is woven from the cultures I’ve lived within. Each place I called home has left an emotional trace — memories, moods, impressions and moments that subtly flow into my designs and I bring these unique global influences into everything I create.
